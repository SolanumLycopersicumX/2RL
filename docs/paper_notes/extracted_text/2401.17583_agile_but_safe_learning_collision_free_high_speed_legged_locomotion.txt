the maximum agility amidst obstacles; 2) Reach-Avoid Value Network (introduced in Section V) is trained to predict the RA values conditioned on the agile

the recovery policy based on the estimated V̂ from the RA value network: 1) if V̂ < Vthreshold , the agile policy is activated to navigate amidst obstacles; 2) if
