D. Training Performance
   To test our method’s improvements in training speed and
efficiency, we trained using both the Base method and our
own method with curriculum learning, recording changes in
terrain level and reward. As shown in Fig. 4, the CMoE
method’s terrain level increased first and reached its maxi-
mum value, indicating that it learned to navigate the most
difficult terrain first. The CMoE method outperformed the
Base method in both reward growth rate and maximum value,
suggesting that the gating network’s terrain classification
reduces learning difficulty and improves efficiency.
